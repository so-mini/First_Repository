No-Knead Focaccia

Prep Time Cooking Time Total Time
20 mins (plus 12~18 hrs) 25 mins 45 mins (+12~18hrs)

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and instant dry yeast.
  2. Add warm water and mix well.
  3. Cover bowl and rest in fridge for 12~18 hours to let it rise.
  4. After letting the dough rise, oil a small mold and transfer dough.
  5. Let dough rise in a warm place for an additional 2 hours.
  6. Add olive oil on dough and sprinkle some salt.
  7. Gently put your fingers on top of the dough to make dimples.
  8. Bake at 200c for 20~25 mins.
  9. Cool and enjoy.
